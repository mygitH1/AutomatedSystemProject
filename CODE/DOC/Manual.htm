Appendix A : USER MANUAL

Web-Enabled Automated Manufacturing Process:

The system creates a web based Manufacturing System that enables a Manufacturing industry to schedule its manufacturing operations based on the daily update of sales from its dealers. Once the sales figures of items for the past week are entered by the Dealers over the internet along with the Orders for the next delivery, the schedule for the next week’s production will be drawn up. A report of the required raw materials or parts will be drawn up with the approved suppliers for each suppliers will be intimated about the product requirements over the internet & asked to quote their rates. Once the rates are quoted, the Order will be placed with the required delivery schedules.

Once the parts are supplied the Stocks will be updated. Then a production plan will be drawn up taking the bill-of-materials into consideration. Once the Production plan is approved, the Stock will be updated when the material is issued. Once the finished products are available the Delivery schedules will be drawn up based on the Orders placed by the Dealers. The stocks with the dealers will also be maintained.

2.1.7 Generate the Production Schedule report

Enter two dates and submit. System generates a Production Schedule Report for the period taking the dates entered into consideration. This report is generated, by calculating the sum of quantity of each product ordered by all the Dealers between the Entered dates & then removing the quantity of that Product already Present in the ware-house above the reorder level for that Product.

This will yield the quantity of the Product that is to be manufactured by the Production department.

2.1.8 Generate the Parts Indent

Click to generate a report of all the parts required for the scheduled production. This will generate the requirement of Parts that are required for the Production of the Quantities arrived at by the Production Schedule report.

These quantities are generated by removing the quantity of Parts above the reorder level, already held with the company in its stores. This will thus generate the final figures for Parts Requirement that have to be ordered from the Suppliers.

2.1.9 Send requests to Suppliers for Quotations

Click to send a mail to all the suppliers requesting for a quotation. This option will retrieve the E-Mail address of the Suppliers & send Mails to them intimating that a Requirement has come up & will request them to Login into the system using the Logins provided to them.

2.1.10 Confirm order for Supply of Parts

Click to generate a list of the quotations from suppliers. Select the lowest or best quotation prices and place orders with the corresponding suppliers. This will confirm the Quotations & change the Status in the Quotations Table to confirmed which will enable you to track which Quotations were confirmed to which Supplier.

2.1.11 Update Part stocks received from Suppliers

Enter quantities of the parts received and submit. This will increase the part stocks in the company stores by the quantity received from supplier.

2.1.12 Update Part stocks delivered to Production Department

Enter quantities of the parts dispatched and submit. This will transfer the parts from the store to the Production department.

2.1.13 Update received stocks of Products

Enter quantities of the products received from the Production department and submit. This will increase the quantity of Products by the amount received from the Production Department.

2.1.14 Dispatch stocks to Dealers

Select from the list of orders placed by dealers and submit. This will dispatch the products in quantity equivalent to the quantity they ordered. The Dealers stocks will be increased by the amount entered & the company’s stocks will be reduced by the amount entered.

2.2 Dealer Options

The figure below shows the Menu options available to the Dealers.

 

2.2.1 Entering Sales figures by the Dealers

Provides two options:

Enter Sales Figures – Enter the sale quantities and click to submit. This will just update the quantities of the Products of the Dealer by reducing the stocks by the quantity entered.

Change Order Quantities – By default system considers the, sale quantities as reorder quantities, which is indicated by checking all the check boxes next to sale quantities. Uncheck the items for which you would like to specify different quantities and click Change Order Quantities to submit.

2.2.2 Placing Orders for Products by the Dealers

This functionality is available in two ways. One of which is from the previous step, i.e., 2.2.1, that provides a way to enter sales figures and submit the same figures or clicking on the change Order Qunatities button. This will take you to a Screen which will allow you to change the quantities. After you make the required changes click the Submit button. This will not only update the sales figures, but also create Orders with the status as "pending".

The second method which is available when the ‘Place Orders’ menu item is clicked, presents a screen where order quantities for each item can be entered and submitted.

2.4.2 Logging out of the System

Click the menu item ‘Logout’. A confirmation dialog appears. If you click on the ‘OK’ button, the state maintained on the server about the User is removed from the session and it logs you off the system. If ‘Cancel’ button is pressed, the User is not logged out but can continue using the system.

For security purposes, be careful to Logout of the system every time you finish using the session.
